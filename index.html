Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr was born on 20 May 1983 to film actor and politician,Nandamuri Harikrishna and Shalini Bhaskar Rao. His father is of Telugu descent and was born and raised at Nimmakuru, in Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh. His mother is a Kannadiga, who hails from Kundapur, Karnataka.He is the grandson of Telugu actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, N. T. Rama Rao.. Initially named Tarak Ram, he was renamed N. T. Rama Rao at the suggestion of his grandfather. Rao did his schooling at Vidyaranya High School, Hyderabad, and completed his intermediate education at St. Mary's College, Hyderabad. He also studied in Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh for some time. Rao is a trained Kuchipudi dancer

About His Family

Rao married Lakshmi Pranathi, the daughter of realtor and businessman Narne Srinivasa Rao.Their wedding took place on May 2011 in Hyderabad at Hitex Exhibition Center, Madhapur.The couple have two sons. Rao is popularly referred to as Young Tiger in Telugu cinema and media.
